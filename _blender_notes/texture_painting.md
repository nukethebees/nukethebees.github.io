## Clearing annotations

Clear annotations by opening the view menu on the right side.
They're under the "Annotations" dropdown.

## Opening the palette

Open the properties sidebar (N).
Tool -> brush settings -> Colour Palette

<figure class="post-figure">
  <img src="/images/blender_notes/texture_painting/palette.webp"
       alt="Colour palette"/>
  <figcaption>
    Colour palette
  </figcaption>
</figure>

## Pack the image

Use `Image -> Pack` to embed the image into the `.blend` file.
